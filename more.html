Caafisom Comprehensive

Within a single, unified platform, CAAFiSOM will support you throughout life's journey to care for your health. Whole-person care addresses everyday health and well-being needs, as well as chronic conditions and acute concerns.

Caafisom Personalized

Data-driven personalization takes into account an individual's socioeconomic background, health and risk factors, and preferences to create a better care experience. By applying these insights, CAAFiSOM whole-person care ensures all individuals feel seen, heard and supported on their own healthcare journey.

Caafisom High-Quality Care

Experienced clinicians, informed by evidence-based care practices and insights about social determinants of health, deliver the highest quality care. Whole-person care fosters health equity and supports the best health outcomes for all people everywhere.

Caafisom Connected

By delivering the right care in the right setting, whole-person care seamlessly combines virtual and in-person care in a hybrid model. Information is exchanged, and clinicians are connected to create the best health outcomes for the patient and reduce the cost of care.

Caafisom Adults

Just like you, your health evolves over time. That's why it's important to get trusted, connected care that focuses on your whole well-being — both body and mind — so you can get healthy, stay healthy and live healthy.

Caafisom Child

Whether your child's caught a bug or a case of the moody blues, we have hospitals with paediatric teams that are available 24/7 to help give your child the care they need, when they need it. Best of all, they can receive top-quality care from the comfort of your home.

Caafisom Senior

When it comes to your health, there's a lot to keep up with. Hospitals with highly experienced doctors and specialists can help address and manage everything from the minor aches and pains that come with aging to skin concerns, general nutrition and chronic conditions that may affect your overall physical and mental well-being.
